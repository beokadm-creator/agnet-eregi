## Design Context

### Users
Korean adults navigating legal and administrative processes — real estate registration, court filings, administrative documents. Mobile-first usage. They access the product during stressful, unfamiliar life events. They need warmth, clarity, and guidance — not corporate coldness.

### Brand Personality
Warm, friendly, approachable. Like a trusted neighborhood professional who makes complexity feel manageable. "Hodu" (호두/walnut) — natural, grounded, reliable.

### Aesthetic Direction
Consumer-grade warmth. Light mode with warm paper tones. Friendly rounded corners. Cognac accent — warm, non-corporate, distinctly Korean consumer feel (closer to Toss/Kakao than SaaS dashboards).

**Brand accent**: Cognac / walnut — `#D87242` (oklch 64% 0.16 55). Warm, friendly, distinctly non-corporate.

**Neutrals**: Warm paper + ink palette. Paper `#FAF8F4` (slight cream), Ink `#0A0A0A`. Hairlines in warm tones `#E9E5DC`. Not stark white/gray.

**Fonts**:
- UI / body: Pretendard Variable — Korean-optimized, clean, friendly, many weights
- Mono: JetBrains Mono — for IDs and technical values

**Anti-references**: SaaS dashboards, corporate blue, stark white minimalism, aggressive fintech UIs.

### Design Principles
1. **Warmth over coldness** — cream paper, warm hairlines, cognac accents. Never stark white/gray.
2. **Friendly roundness** — generous radii (8-28px), rounded buttons, pill badges. Approachable, not corporate.
3. **Weighty buttons** — 44-64px height buttons with bold weight. Toss-inspired confidence in CTAs.
4. **One action at a time** — progressive disclosure over information overload.
5. **No AI tells** — no gradient text, no side-stripe borders, no glassmorphism, no generic card grids.
